International Conference on emerging trends in Mechanical and Materials Engineering (ICMME ‘21) is organized by the Department of Mechanical Engineering under the aegis of Christ Engineering Congress – ‘21. ICMME – ‘21 aims to bring together researchers, engineers, and scholar students to share their experiences, ideas, research and discuss the challenges encountered and the most optimal solutions.

Original extended asbstract submissions from prospective authors are invited in the following template. Send your completed submissions toicice2021@cce.edu.in.

Topics

ICCMME-’21 will ensure deliberations on a bunch of related topics which include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Thermal Sciences and Transport Phenomena
  • Material Science and Manufacturing Technology
  • Mechanical Design, Vibration and Tribology
  • Renewable Energy and Alternate Fuels
  • Micro and Nano-Technology
  • Metrology and Instrumentation
  • Fluid flow and fluid power systems
  • Industrial Engineering and Operations management
  • Mechatronics, Robotics and Automation
  • Reliability and Maintenance Engineering
  • Numerical Models and Computational Methods
  • Inter-disciplinary Engineering and allied topics
