Kerala's First Multi International Conference Exclusively for Engineering Students
7-9 July 2021
EXPLORE ABROAD

About the Event

Explore Abroad is an event to explore the opportunities in abroad for UG/PG students. This event will provide a comprehensive idea about the job and higher studies opportunities, requirements to apply for abroad opportunities, how to procced with formal application procedures, etc. These sessions will be handled by experts who have already placed in companies or institutions in abroad, especially from Germany, UK, Canada and Singapore. By attending this session, the participants can make their abroad dream come true. The list of resource persons handling this session will be updated soon.
