International Conference on Circuits, Control and Energy -2021 (ICCCE-'21) will be hosted by Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda, Thrissur, Kerala, India during 7th - 9th of July 2021. ICCCE-‘21 is mainly organized for the Graduate Students, Post Graduate Students, Research Scholars and Faculty members to contribute in the field of electrical engineering. The main goal of this conference is to bring out innovative ideas on emerging technologies, and to have an interaction between the students, academicians and practicing engineers.

Original extended asbstract submissions from prospective authors are invited in the following template. Send your completed submissions toicice2021@cce.edu.in.
