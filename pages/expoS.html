CHRIST ENGINEERING CONGRESS 2021 PROJECT EXPO – SCHOOL STUDENTS

Kerala's First Multi International Conference Exclusively for Engineering Students
7-9 July 2021

About the Event

Project Expo is a technical event for school students, conducted as a part of Christ Engineering Congress 2021 (CEC 2021). By participating in this event, school students will get an opportunity to show case their developed projects or demonstrations of theories/principles to the society, industrialists or other stake holders. Such opportunity can invite more opportunities participating in events conducted at state/national/international levels, like school science expos, exhibitions, etc. It also helps to ignite other aspiring minds.

How to Participate

To participate in Project Expo, the participating team/student should setup their project or demonstration at their premises and make a video with narration to demonstrate the working model for not more than five minutes. The narration must be preferably in English. The physical presence of one of the students in the video is mandatory. After making the video, upload in YouTube and send us the YouTube link. This video will be considered for the evaluation purpose. While sending the video, a photograph of the project also has to be attached along with Abstract of the project/demonstration, not exceeding 300 words.

Evaluation Criteria

There will be a preliminary screening of all submitted videos by the expert panel for the final round. The selected videos for the final round will be presented through online platform during the Project Expo event time on 9th July 2021. During this time, another expert panel, will be evaluating the submitted videos. By this evaluation, best projects or demonstrations will be awarded with first, second and third prizes. All submitted projects or demonstrations are eligible to receive participation certificate for the team members.

General Rules and Guidelines for Competition

  • Each team can have maximum of 3 members
  • The competition is open to regular school students
  • A team member can participate in only one submission
  • The video must be of length not more than five minutes
  • Send us your submissions to cec2021@cce.edu.in with subject “Project Expo- School Level – Your Team Name” on or before 20th June 2021
  • The project submission should contain,
    • Abstract of the Project or demonstration with maximum 300 words
    • An authorization letter from your college or all team members College ID photos
    • All team members contact details (Mobile Number and E-mail ID) with Name
    • YouTube link of the working model video
    • A photograph of the working model
  • Mode of evaluation will be ONLINE
  • The Judging panel decision will be final for the declaration of winners
