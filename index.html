About Christ College of Engineering

Christ College of Engineering (CCE) was established in 2015 in the heart of Irinjalakuda town for providing high-quality professional education in the field of Engineering and Technology. The college is managed by the CMI Fathers of Devamatha Province, Thrissur, and is part of Christ Educational and Charitable Trust, Irinjalakuda. We are currently offering Bachelor of Technology programs in 5 fields:

  • Electronics & Communication Engineering  
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering  
  • Computer Science Engineering  
  • Mechanical Engineering  
  • Civil Engineering  

We are one of the top five best performing self-financing institutions under A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University, Thiruvananthapuram, right from our inception. We provide extra add-on courses, hands-on training sessions and socially committed co-curricular activities to our students in addition to the regular university curriculum, for nurturing them as socially committed engineering professionals.

About CEC 21

Christ Engineering Congress 2021 (CEC 2021) is one of its first ever exclusive comprehensive programme for engineering students over the globe for show casing their technical excellence through various technical events. CEC 2021 delivers a platform for engineering students for the dissemination of innovative concepts, thoughts and end level products or solutions. The programme is organized by Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda, Thrissur, Kerala, on virtual platform during 7th to 9th July 2021. CEC 2021 is meant to bridge the gap in curricular and co-curricular activities of engineering students happened due to the existing pandemic situation. Hence, the various events under CEC 2021 are framed according to meet the opportunities required by the engineering students across the world. CEC 2021 will host invited talks, online conferences, project expos, industry @ live, panel discussions, etc. for the benefit of participants in the field of engineering and technology.
